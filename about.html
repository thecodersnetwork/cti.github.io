OUR PRODUCTS

What We Do?

CTI specializes in saw milling and supplying high quality, special sizes of timber on demand, within short lead times. Utilizing our state-of-the-art sawmill, we produce standard as well as custom special sizes and offer a range of services such as: heavy carpentry, fabrication of bespoke timber components, kiln drying, to trading in sawn timber and a plethora of other forest products.

  • Certified Deciduous Hardwood Sawn Timber.

    We source and supply quality deciduous hardwoods like Oak, Beech, Maple, Walnut, Ash etc. from Europe and North America.

  • Certified Hardwood & Softwood Round Logs.

    We import for our own consumption as well as trade in certified hardwood and softwood round logs from various sources spread across the globe.

  • Certified Tropical Hardwood.

    CTI mills and processes custom cut certified tropical hardwoods for various applications and end uses ranging from - external heavy duty, marine to interior applications. We process hardwood species such as: Azobe (Ekki), Bilinga, Greenheart, Iroko, Red Balau, Yellow Balau & Teak.

  • Coniferous Softwood.

    We stock and supply Softwoods (Whitewood) such as Spruce and Pine for packaging and construction purposes. We process softwood species such as: Douglas Fir, Pine & Spruce.

  • Decking, Crane Mats, Skid Mats, & Fenders.

    We produce heavy duty decking for Ships, Barges and Offshore Platform, as well as marinas and resorts. We possess inhouse capabilities to fabricate bespoke heavy duty timber crane mats, skid mats and fenders in accordance with our clients specifications.

  • Custom Milled Special Sizes.

    Our state of art factory enables to produce custom milled special sizes, in accordance with our clients requirements.

  • Furniture & Center Pieces.

    We produce specialty slabs for table and counter tops and a range of special timber center pieces for displays for various spaces including indoor and outdoor.
