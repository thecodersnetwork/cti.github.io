CTI PRODUCTS #4

Round Logs

CTI imports and trades in a wide variety of Tropical Hardwood and Coniferous round logs. We work through sustainable channels to source and supply various species of logs from across the globe.

CTI encourages all our supply chain partners to conduct and operate on sustainable harvesting methodologies such as selective felling in far flung forests and plantations, that is based on a highly scientific and methodological approach which our team advocates for based on their years of operational experience in the field.

Our proprietary ERP system enables us to log and capture all raw material that is fed into our production line, and helps us carefully monitor each stage of production, from optimized selection of logs for each order, milling to grading and assessing final recovery. Our system is designed to provide us with predictive analytics that reduce waste and maximizes our recovery.
